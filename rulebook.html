WHO CAN AVAIL THESE SERVICES AND CRITERIA OFSCRUTINISING?

If a person falls in any of the following categories, then he/she can avail of free Legal Aid at Kartavyam:


1- Is a person belonging to the Below Poverty Line (BPL) category; or Is a person with an annual income of Rs 2,50,000
2- Members of socially and economically deprived sections; who is a widow; Who is a transgender; Who is a sex worker
3- Is a woman, and whose income falls within the above income bracket (however cases other than that bracket can be included given the discretionary decision of Kartavyam.) Is a disabled person; Is a lunatic person;
4- Is a victim of human trafficking, forced labor (beggar), violence mass disaster, hate crime, environmental disasters; Is a child, i.e., below the age of 18 years, Is a transgender;
5- Is an ex-serviceman;
